Couscous
2 hr • Serves 6

Ingredients
  • lamb stew, couscous semolina, onions, garlic, tomato paste, harissa, spices, vegetables
Instructions
  1. Fry aromatics. Add meat & spices. Simmer with vegetables for 1 hour.
  2. Prepare semolina as package instructs. Serve meat & vegetables over couscous.
Shakshuka
30 min • Serves 4-5
Ingredients & steps▾
Ingredients
  • olive oil, onion, peppers, garlic, tomatoes, cumin, paprika, eggs
Instructions
  1. Sauté vegetables, add tomatoes and spices. Make craters and crack eggs. Cover until eggs set.
  2. Serve with crusty bread.
Lablabi
2 hr • Serves 4
Ingredients & steps▾
Ingredients
  • dried chickpeas, garlic, cumin, caraway, harissa, lemon, olive oil, stale bread
Instructions
  1. Soak chickpeas. Cook until tender. Prepare seasoning and poached eggs. Assemble over bread and ladle broth.
